SaaS and Mobile Solutions

Our mobile and web based software solutions are custom designed for each client. Each project starts with a customer description of the problem they are trying to solve. They have multiple employees who hand gather data at a variety of offsite locations. At the end of the day, it’s turned in for someone to manually enter into their proprietary computer system.

They had four problems to solve:

  • Easy to use interface
  • Instant access to information
  • Better accuracy and efficiency
  • Minimize internal software changes

We recommended a 3-Tier approach:

  • Mobile Survey Application with separate designs optimized for the iPhone and iPad.
  • A cloud based Ruby on Rails server to handle syncing questions and answers with JSON.
  • A thin layer internal interface to translate the questions and answers to and from their existing format.

Absolute Simplicity

To keep our customer’s job simple we provided:

  • Complete project management and schedule tracking
  • User story development and requirement gathering
  • Application design and development
  • Full testing with a documented test suite
  • Consultation on creating the internal interface
  • Full deployment and hosting

In the end, our customer’s job was to load their new application and run a more productive business.
